Chukwuemeka Odumegwu Ojukwu University, Anambra state

Chukwuemeka Odumegwu Ojukwu University formerly Anambra State University is a Nigerian tertiary institution located in Uli, a town in Anambra State. It has its permanent site in Igbariam in Anambra East local government area under the Anambra North Senatorial District. It also has a campus in Awka for medical students.
