Pharmacy is a course of study which produces professionals in the field of drug manufacturing, production, componding, distribution and use. It is act, practice or profession of preparing drugs according to Doctor's prescription. The pharmacy profession is cordinated by the body Pharmacists Council of Nigeria (PCN).
This course is offered by over 20 universities in Nigeria. My school, Chukwuemeka Odumegwu Ojukwu University , Anambra state happens to be one of such higher institutions and has its Faculty of Pharmaceutical Sciences situated at the Igbariam campus.
We have six Departments that make up the faculty of pharmaceutical sciences in my faculty.



Importance of pharmacy profession

Pharmacy profession breeds experts who play an important role in helping patients get the best results from their medications. Pharmacists are medication experts who enhance patient care and promote. They posses a greater level of knowledge about medications and therefore provide information on them to patients and other health care workers.


Patients do best when pharmacists are part of their healthcare teams because pharmacists are the medication specialists. Pharmacists improve medication adherence. They educate patients on the dosage & timing of the medicine because this ensures quick recovery.
If mankind has to survive, we require skilled pharmacists because they can protect health through innovative drugs vital for life.



Fields in Pharmacy

There are many different types of pharmacy, and other places where a trained pharmacist may work. This includes:

  • Community pharmacy
  • Hospital pharmacy
  • Clinical pharmacy
  • Industrial pharmacy
  • Compounding pharmacy
  • Consulting pharmacy
  • Ambulatory care pharmacy
  • Regulatory pharmacy
  • Home care pharmacy


Personal qualities of a good pharmacist:

  • Learner
